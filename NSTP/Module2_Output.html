Evacuation Plan, Go Bag, and Vegetable Garden

Evacuation Plan – Each learner needs to draw an evacuation plan of their own house or community. Work output should be placed in ⅛ size of an illustration board with full description. Output should be displayed in an accessible location within the learner’s house. Take a picture and submit in PDF file.
Emergency GO Bag – the learner should prepare an Emergency GO Bag for him or her which should be placed in an accessible location within the learner’s house. Each item should be identified and explain why such is included inside the Go bag. A written description should be submitted in PDF file.
Vegetable Garden – the learner should start planting vegetables (of any kind) in at least 5 empty bottles of soda (1.5 liters). Growing of vegetables will be monitored until fully grown. Initial result should be shown through pictures.
